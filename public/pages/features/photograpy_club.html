Photograpy Club

The Rangamati Science & Technology University Photography Club is a creative community dedicated to capturing moments, telling stories, and expressing artistic visions through the lens of a camera. Our club welcomes photographers of all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals, who share a passion for photography and visual storytelling.
Through regular meetings, workshops, and photo outings, our members have the opportunity to develop their technical skills, explore different photographic styles and genres, and share their work with fellow enthusiasts. Whether you’re interested in landscape photography, portrait photography, street photography, or experimental photography, there’s something for everyone in our club.
Our achievements showcase the talent and dedication of our members. From winning awards in local and national photography competitions to exhibiting their work in galleries and online platforms, our photographers have demonstrated their creativity and skill on numerous occasions.
Joining the Rangamati Science & Technology University Photography Club offers members the chance to learn from each other, collaborate on projects, and be part of a supportive and inspiring community. Whether you’re looking to improve your photography skills, meet like-minded individuals, or simply share your love for photography, we invite you to join us and embark on a journey of creative exploration and discovery.
