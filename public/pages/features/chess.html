Chess Club

About Us
Welcome to the Rangamati Science and Technology University Chess Club! Our club is a dynamic and inclusive community for chess enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, the Rangamati Science and Technology University Chess Club offers a supportive environment where everyone can enjoy and improve their chess skills.
Mission Statement
Our mission is to promote the game of chess within the university, providing opportunities for skill development, competitive play, and intellectual engagement. We aim to foster a spirit of strategic thinking, sportsmanship, and camaraderie among our members.
Membership
Membership is open to all Rangamati Science and Technology University students, regardless of experience level. We also welcome faculty, staff, and alumni who are interested in participating. Our club prides itself on its inclusivity and community spirit..
