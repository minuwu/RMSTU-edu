Debate Club

The Rangamati Science & Technology University Debating Club is a vibrant community where students come together to engage in lively discussions, hone their public speaking skills, and explore diverse perspectives on a wide range of topics. As one of the university's oldest and most esteemed organizations, we have a rich tradition of fostering critical thinking, effective communication, and respectful discourse among our members.
Through regular debates, workshops, and tournaments, our club provides members with opportunities to develop their argumentation skills, research abilities, and confidence in public speaking. Whether you're passionate about politics, ethics, social issues, or current events, there's a place for you in the Debating Club to voice your opinions and defend your ideas.
Our achievements speak volumes about the caliber of our debaters. From winning national championships to representing our university in prestigious debating competitions around the world, our members consistently demonstrate their intellectual prowess and persuasive abilities on the debate stage.
Joining the Rangamati Science & Technology University Debating Club offers members the chance to expand their horizons, sharpen their analytical skills, and build lifelong friendships with fellow debaters. Whether you're a seasoned debater or new to the world of debating, we invite you to join us and become part of our tradition of excellence in reasoned argumentation and civil discourse.
